Hey, I'm Koby 👋🏼
I create digital experiences that allow users to more easily understand and improve their physical worlds. As a Product Designer at Foursquare, I integrate mapping and data visualization on our enterprise platforms to make complex information insightful and actionable.
I've lived former lives as an architect, urban designer, GIS specialist, and front-end engineer and have collaborated on a range of projects including climate resilience planning at AECOM, infectious disease control at The Broad Institute, and in-hospital technologies at Mayo Clinic. I received my Masters from Harvard University's Graduate School of Design and a BS in Architecture from Washington University in St. Louis.
