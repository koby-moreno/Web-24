In response to COVID-19, community leaders needed quick and clear data to inform on-the-fly social policies. Fathom collaborated with Colorado Mesa University (CMU) to develop Lookout, a multi-dashboard platform designed to monitor and track campus outbreaks. In my role as a UX Engineer, I utilized spatial data to prototype and implement new visualization solutions, enabling our client to run hybrid classes and expanding our agency's information design capabilities.
